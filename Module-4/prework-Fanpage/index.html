Glass Animals is a Great Band

Bio

In 2014, the band released their debut album, ZABA. Spotify noticed their rising potential, with the (now platinum) single "Gooey" and landed a top 2 spot on their global and UK viral charts at the end of the year. Having built up a devoted fan base following their second album, "How To Be A Human Being," Glass Animals garnered mainstream acclaim.

Glass Animals carries the same lyrical sound, but constantly expirements with new sounds, creating amazing instrumentation.

Album Information

  • Their first album, Zaba , feels like an adventure through the jungle.
  • Their second album, How to Be a Human Being is a collection of stories from their fans. And it is full of even more bombastic tracks.
